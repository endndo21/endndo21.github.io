Location

The Tiger is very common in Afghanistan, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Iran, Pakistan and Singapore.

The tiger feeds on:

  1. 1) Wild boar
  2. 2) Deer
  3. 3) Monkeys
  4. 3) Fish

Information

The tiger is the largest cat in the cat family.
The tiger is a predator and feeds mainly on meat.
It stays in warm places and stays where there is more food.
